Service • Ownership • Achievement • Respect

Service

Definition: SERVICE means providing help or support to someone or something.

Example: Helping someone with their groceries when they're struggling to carry multiple bags.

How I Apply It: Helping classmates understand technical concepts, assisting with technology at school events, and serving as a teacher's assistant.

Ownership

Definition: OWNERSHIP means acknowledging and accepting responsibility for the consequences of your actions.

Example: Admitting to making a mistake and taking steps to correct it, rather than blaming others. For example, I broke a dish and acknowledged the mistake by apologizing.

How I Apply It: Taking responsibility for project outcomes, acknowledging mistakes in code, being accountable for deadlines, and accepting feedback constructively.

Achievement

Definition: ACHIEVEMENT is the act of accomplishing something or achieving a goal.

Example: Earning an honor roll certificate for academic excellence.

My Achievements: Maintaining high academic performance, completing complex programming projects, developing quantum computing skills, and earning professional certifications.

Respect

Definition: RESPECT means showing consideration and appreciation for someone or something.

Example: Listening carefully when someone is speaking and valuing their opinions, even if you disagree.

How I Apply It: Listening actively to teachers and peers, respecting different perspectives, being punctual, treating school property with care, and acknowledging team contributions.
