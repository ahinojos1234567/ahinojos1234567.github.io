High School Student

I'm Armando, a high school student with a passion for quantum mechanics and technology. At TPA, I've had the opportunity to grow both academically and personally, taking on challenges that have shaped my understanding of science and technology. The school has provided me with resources, supportive teachers, and a community that encourages exploration. As a teacher's assistant, I've learned the value of helping others succeed. TPA has taught me the importance of balance, dedication, and perseverance, which have become essential parts of who I am.

My future plans involve pursuing a degree in physics or computer science with a focus on quantum computing. I want to contribute to research in quantum algorithms and eventually work at a research institution or tech company at the forefront of quantum technology. The values instilled by my family and reinforced through my experiences at TPA guide everything I do. They've taught me that success isn't just about individual achievement, but about contributing to something larger and lifting others up along the way.
